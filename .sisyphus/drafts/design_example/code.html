Improving Campus Lighting & Security in South Parking

상세 내용

Students have reported significant concerns regarding safety near the south parking lot due to inadequate lighting at night. Several light posts have been flickering or are completely out for the past two weeks. This area is heavily used by students leaving late-night lab sessions. We request immediate replacement of bulbs and a review of the lighting density in that zone.
